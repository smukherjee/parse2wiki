**What makes the team section succeed:** Named individuals with specific, relevant experience. Commitment statements. Realistic ramp-up timelines. Retention evidence.

**What makes the team section fail:** "TBD" for key personnel. Generic role descriptions. Unrealistic ramp-up (full team on day one when hiring is required). No retention strategy.

**What makes the pricing section succeed:** Transparent methodology. Rates benchmarked against market or schedule. Cost assumptions stated so the evaluator can assess realism. Value narrative tied to evidence.

**What makes the pricing section fail:** Unsupported rates. No methodology explanation. Ignoring cost elements the RFP requires. Loss-leader pricing that the evaluator does not believe is sustainable.
